# Mini Guitar Notices

## WebAudioFont

The sampled acoustic guitar tone uses WebAudioFont Player and WebAudioFontData assets vendored in `vendor/webaudiofont/`.

Sources:
- WebAudioFont Player: https://github.com/surikov/webaudiofont
- WebAudioFontData: https://github.com/surikov/webaudiofontdata
- Acoustic steel preset: `0250_LK_AcousticSteel_SF2_file.js`

License files are included in `vendor/webaudiofont/`.
